## 📋 Overview

SMART-SB is a web-based application designed to improve the **accessibility**, **efficiency**, and **transparency** of legislative document retrieval for the Sangguniang Bayan of Santa Cruz, Davao del Sur — and its constituents.

The system bridges the gap between local government legislative processes and public access by providing a centralized, modern, and user-friendly platform for managing and retrieving official records.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📂 **Centralized Document Repository** — All legislative records stored and organized in one place
- 🔍 **Advanced Search & Filtering** — Quickly locate ordinances, resolutions, and other documents
- 🔓 **Public Access** — Constituents can access and view legislative documents with ease
- 📱 **Responsive Design** — Works seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- 🏷️ **Document Categorization** — Organized by type, date, session, and legislative term
- 🔒 **Secure & Reliable** — Built with data integrity and accessibility standards in mind
- 📊 **Transparency Dashboard** — Promotes open governance through readily available records

---

## 🎯 Purpose

This system was developed to address the challenges in accessing legislative documents from the local government unit of Santa Cruz, Davao del Sur. Prior to SMART-SB, retrieving official records required physical visits or manual coordination with the Sangguniang Bayan office.

With SMART-SB, **any constituent** can now access official records anytime, anywhere — upholding the principles of transparency and good governance.
